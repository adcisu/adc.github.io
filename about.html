ABOUT THE COLLECTIVE

A living archive of architectural thought.

The Architecture Documentation Collective is a student-run archive dedicated to documenting the lived experience of architectural education. We believe that the process — the sketches, the late nights, the site visits, the failed models — is as important as the final product.

OUR MISSION

Architecture school is a transformative experience. ADC exists to capture that transformation across all years — from the first year learning to draft, to the fifth year synthesizing a lifetime of spatial thinking.

By documenting field studies, building analyses, experimental projects, and reflections, we build a collective memory that future students can learn from and contribute to.
